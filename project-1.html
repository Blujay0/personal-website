Weatherly

A short subtitle

The Weatherly app is a user-friendly, serverless web application designed to provide up-to-the-minute weather information for any city. With a sleek and intuitive interface, users can effortlessly search for their desired location, and Weatherly delivers comprehensive weather forecasts, including local time, date, current conditions, and temperature in degrees Celsius. This app ensures that users are always in the know about the weather, making it an indispensable tool for planning daily activities or staying updated on weather conditions worldwide. With Weatherly, staying informed and prepared for any weather is just a click away.
